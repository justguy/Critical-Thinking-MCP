## Response

```text
30-day plan:

Week 1: Listen and map
- Meet each person 1:1 for 30 minutes. Ask: what helps you do your best work, when do you feel left out, what should change first.
- Audit team rituals: who speaks, who gets context late, who is left off decisions, who is overloaded.
- Set one explicit norm immediately: no important decisions in side conversations without written recap to the full team.

Week 2: Fix team habits
- Start a weekly team update with priorities, decisions, risks, and shout-outs.
- In meetings, rotate facilitation, invite quieter voices first, and end with clear owners and notes shared to everyone.
- Create a simple inclusion rule: information is shared by default, not by request.

Week 3: Build morale visibly
- Remove 1-2 concrete frustrations the team raised.
- Recognize good work publicly and specifically.
- Balance work fairly and check that growth opportunities are not going to the same people.

Week 4: Measure and adjust
- Run a short anonymous pulse survey: “I feel included,” “I know what’s going on,” “My manager listens,” “Team morale is improving.”
- Review results with the team, name what changed, and commit to the next 2 fixes.

Non-negotiables for you
- No favorites.
- No surprises.
- No public blame.
- Follow through quickly on small commitments.

- Everyone has had a 1:1.
- Team decisions are documented.
- Meeting participation is more balanced.
```
